November 2009 - TEEJI Open Day

TEEJI Engineering Company held its Open Day last November 4, 2009 in Bustan's Conference Center (www.bustanhotel.com). The event was organized by TEEJI Engineering to formally introduce the newly established company to prospective clients in the Libyan Oil & Gas sector. A number of representatives from various Oil & Gas companies were present during the event.

TEEJI Engineering's CEO, Mr. Simon Berdai, welcomed the guests with an introductory speech. Managing Director, Mr. Giulio Giannoli, followed with a presentation containing relevant information regarding TEEJI. A copy of this presentation can be downloaded here.

TEEJI's Technical Services Manager, Mr. Edward Martin, led the guests for a tour around the office facilities. The event ended with a sumptuous lunch at Bustan's Restaurant.

The Open Day was a successful event for TEEJI and will hopefully have “opened” our doors to successful relationships with our clients.
