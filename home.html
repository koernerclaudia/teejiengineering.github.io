TEEJI ENGINEERING

... is a Libyan Company registered under the Libyan joint venture law.

As the need for Engineering services in Libya is on the increase, LCM Oil & Gas and its partner decided to create a new entity in this line of service. Today, TEEJI Engineering is one of the few joint Engineering companies with industrial experience that is fully operational and 100% based in Libya.

The company objectives are the following:

Rendering Engineering Services and technical works pertaining with the maintenance of power distribution network for the industrial and constructional projects
Rendering Engineering works, computer programs and technical training to the companies operating in Oil & Gas exploration and development field
