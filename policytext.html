POLICIES

Teeji Engineering is committed to operational excellence, complying with the highest Quality, Environmental and Health and Safety International standards.

Guiding principles of excellence commitment is to evaluate client satisfaction, effectiveness of performance, wellbeing of Teeji employees, Subcontractors, and all the others stakeholders, and environment.
Excellence objectives can be reached operating in compliance with responsible management practices, stated to minimize every non conformance or adverse impact on process or product Quality, on Environment and on Health and Safety arising from Teeji activities.

Teeji Engineering implemented, maintains and continuously improves an integrated Quality, Environment and Health and Safety management system, based on international standards ISO9001, ISO14001, OHSAS 18001.

Guiding principles of TEEJI Engineering's Management system are:

Insuring the compliance with laws and regulations and requisition and with mentioned international standards
Identifying and managing risks to as low as reasonably practicable when they can affect quality of services or product, or they can cause an accident, injury or illness to people, physical or psychological or they can have a negative impact on environment
Stating objectives to reach to continue improve of quality, environment and Health and Safety performance.
Participation in implementing the present Policy by all the employees, suppliers, contractors, business partners, customers and authority
Communicating expectations and/or training people working with or on behalf of Teeji Engineering to ensure that they are made aware of their individual responsibility and of the consequences of their actions.

Teeji Engineering's policy will be reviewed at least every year within the Management System Review process and shall be re-issued in case of relevant changes in Company activity, dimensions or risks or even more often if the Management reviews highlight the need of it.
